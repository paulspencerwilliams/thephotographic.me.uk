Tent Pegs? Sorted!!

May 16th, 2007

Spoke to Terra Nova and The Outdoor Shop today to sort out knackered carbon fibre tent pegs.

Terra Nova wouldn’t move from their default ‘we can replace like with like or offer a downgrade to aluminium pegs’ - not good. Luckily The Outdoor Shop was a little more sympathetic. They hadn’t heard of other stories of these problems but agreed in principle in replacing the pegs with like for like - including same price replacement. As the only good lightweight skewers offered by Terra Nova were the Titanium Skewers, they offered me these provided I pay the difference - normally nothing, but their discount price means I have to pay Â£6.02. I am not too peeved but think they could have offered them for nothing.

Still, hopefully next I should be the proud owner of some more titanium to go with my MSR cutlery set. Oh yeah, and I go to the lakes in only, er, 45 hours or so:)
