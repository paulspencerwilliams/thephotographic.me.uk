Weekend Without Walking :(

Mar 31st, 2007

Not going out as last weekend I went to Wales (Hay Bluff / Lord Hereford’s Knob) and promptly knackered my ankle then my knee. This time my right knee feels how my left knee felt over Christmas. This isn’t good as I only have 3 weeks to mend myself before start of Project Peaks. Trying to sort physio over next couple of days and might hold back on walking next weekend too - not good as it is Easter. At least my new Paramo Fuera is GREAT.
